PepeXchange Sales Mechanism

The PepeX sales mechanism is in several stages, the first stage is Seed Sales. Seed sales are the initial stage of offering tokens, in this stage we offer tokens at very cheap prices. And the second stage is the Private Sale stage. In this stage we offer tokens at a different price compared to the Seed Sale. The third stage is the Presale stage, in this stage the tokens we offer are more expensive than in the previous stage. And the final stage we offer tokens through the IDO platform in collaboration with our senior DEX OpBNB, here we hold the IDO on our senior DEX platform.

We offer this initial sale allocating in the development of the project and the advancement of the ecosystem, and a sense of shared solidarity with the PepeXchange community. Below are more complete details about the sales mechanism organized by PepeXchange.



Stage 1: Seed Sale


Join our Seed Sale, a chance to be part of the project from the start. The following are details of the total supply of token allocations in the Seed Sale stage.

Supply Pool 10,000,000,000,000 tokens.

With an average price of 250,000,000,000 tokens per 1 BNB.

Minimum purchase is 0.1 BNB.

Maximum purchase of 10 BNB for each contributor.

Soft Cap 20 BNB.

Hard Cap 40 BNB.

Place of Sale: On the official PepeX website



Stage 2: Private Sales


The second private stage after the Seed sale, at the Private Sale we offer a different price difference to the Seed Sale, here are the details about the Private Sale.

Private Sale supply pool 10,000,000,000,000 tokens.

Average price 220,000,000,000 tokens per 1 BNB.

Minimum purchase is 0.1 BNB.

Maximum purchase of 12 BNB for each Contributor.

Soft Cap 22 BNB.

Hard Cap 46 BNB.

Place of Sale: On the official PepeX website



Stage 3: Presale


In the Presale stage, the difference in token prices offered is very different from the Seed Sale or Private Sale. Following are the details about Presale.

Supply pool 10,000,000,000,000 tokens.

With an average price of 180,000,000,000 tokens per 1 BNB.

Minimum purchase is 0.1 BNB.

Maximum purchase is 13 BNB.

Soft Cap 27 BNB.

Hard Cap 56 BNB.

Place of sale: On the official PepeX website



Stage 4: IDO (Initial DEX Offering)


In the final stage we collaborated on an IDO with our senior DEX OpBNB, in this stage the prices we proposed were very different, here are the details about the IDO.

Supply pool allocation of 200,000,000,000,000 tokens.

With an average price of 120,000,000,000 tokens per 1 BNB.

Soft Cap 116 BNB.

Hard Cap 166 BNB.

Place of sale: At DEX OpBNB




That's all the details and our sales mechanism, with this policy we pay attention to the entire community both connected to us and around the OpBNB ecosystem, we really appreciate your appreciation and attention, greetings from us.
