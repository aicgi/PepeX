How to Remove Liquidity?

Removing liquidity from a pool on PepeXchange is an important step if you want to withdraw your assets from the pool. Here is a step by step guide on how to clear liquidity in PepeXchange.



Step 1: Access PepeXchange


• Open the PepeXchange website in your web browser and make sure you are connected to a wallet that has liquidity in the pool you want to delete.



Step 2: Select a Liquidity Pool


• On the PepeXchange home page, look for the “Liquidity Pool” option.

• Click to enter the liquidity pool page.



Step 3: Select the Pool you want to remove liquidity from


• On the liquidity pools page, you will see the different pools available.

• Select the pool that has the liquidity you want to delete.

• For example, if you want to remove liquidity from an BNB/PEPEX pool, select that pool.



Step 4: Click "Remove Liquidity"


• On the selected pool page, look for the “Remove Liquidity” button or a similar option and click on it.



Step 5: Enter the Amount of Liquidity to be Removed


• PepeXchange will ask you to enter the amount of liquidity you want to remove from the pool.

• Enter the amount according to what you want.



Step 6: Confirm Transaction


• After entering the liquidity amount, PepeXchange will display the details of the transaction you will make.

• Make sure to check these details carefully.

• Then, confirm the transaction with your wallet.



Step 7: Wait for Confirmation


• Wait until the transaction is confirmed on the blockchain network.

• This may take a while depending on the level of network congestion.



Step 8: Liquidity Removed


• Once the transaction is confirmed, your liquidity will be removed from the pool, and your assets will be returned to your wallet.




Vital Records:

Make sure you have enough gas to complete the liquidity removal transaction.

Removal of liquidity will reduce your rights to trading fees and trading results in that pool.

By following the steps above, you can remove liquidity from the appropriate pool. Make sure to always check the transaction details and the gas fees charged before confirming them.
