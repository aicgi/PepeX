How To Swap In PepeXchange?

To make a swap on PepeXchange you can follow these steps



Step 1: Open DEX


• Go to the PepeXchange main page.



Step 2: Connect Wallet


• Make sure your crypto wallet is connected to PepeXchange. Select a suitable wallet and follow the instructions that appear to connect it.



Step 3: Select Assets


• Select the crypto asset you want to swap. For example, you want to exchange (BNB) for (PEPEX).



Step 4: Set Amount"


• Determine the amount of assets you want to swap. Make sure you have sufficient balance in your wallet to execute the swap.



Step: 5 Swap


• Click the "Swap" button. PepeXchange will display transaction details, including the gas fee required.



Step 6: Confirm


• After checking the transaction details, confirm your swap. You may need to approve transactions in your crypto wallet.

• Wait a few moments until the transaction is successfully completed. This may take a few seconds or more depending on network conditions.

• After a successful swap, you will see your balance has been updated in your wallet.




Vital Records:

Make sure you have a crypto wallet connected to the PepeXchange network you wish to use such as MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and other recommended EVM wallets, and have an appropriate token balance in your wallet before performing the swap.

Note that gas fees may vary depending on network traffic levels.
