About

Welcome to PepeX, where memes meet DeFi on the OPBNB & Multi-Chain EVM network. We are a decentralized trading platform (DEX) that allows you to experience the thrill of memes while participating in safe and efficient crypto asset trading.

PepeX is a crypto coin inspired by the Pepe the Frog meme and powered by blockchain technology on the OPBNB network. We offer a unique and exciting trading experience while maintaining security and transparency.

Roadmap

  • Phase I

  • Development: Building the foundation of PepeX, including smart contracts for the exchange and its technical infrastructure.
  • Alpha Test: Internal testing and alpha development of the platform version.
  • Security Audits: Conduct external security audits to ensure the security of the PepeX platform.
  • Alpha Launch: Launch of the alpha version of the PepeX platform for testing by internal teams and stakeholders.

  • Phase II

  • Public Beta Launch: Public beta launch for access for the community.
  • Airdrop Program: Launch of the Airdrop program for communities that play an active role.
  • Official Launch: Official public launch of PepeX across multiple DEX mainnet for all users.

  • Phase III

  • Ecosystem Development: Expanding the PepeX ecosystem with communities and projects.
  • DeFi Application Development: Opening the door to PepeX-based DeFi application development.
  • Market Expansion: Opens access to more trading pairs and crypto assets.
  • Community Development: More active promotional and community development activities.

  • Phase IV

  • Research and Development: Continuing with innovative research to improve technology and user experience.
  • Product Diversification: Offer innovative products, such as NFTs and derivatives.
  • Expansion To Other Markets: Exploring the possibility of expanding to other networks outside of OPBNB.
  • Partnerships and Collaboration: Build strategic partnerships for faster growth and adoption.
  • Regular Updates: Continuously release updates and fixes based on user feedback.
