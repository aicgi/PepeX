How To Connect Wallet On PepeXchange?

In this tutorial, we will provide a simple guide on how to connect your wallet device to PepeXchange and setup the blockchain network accordingly. We will provide an example setup for MetaMask, one of the popular web crypto wallets.



Step 1: Download and Install MetaMask


• If you don't have MetaMask, download and install the MetaMask app or extension on your device.

• Follow the instructions on the official MetaMask website to set up your account.



Step 2: Import or Create Wallet


• If you have an existing MetaMask wallet, simply import your wallet with your existing recovery phrase.

• If you don't have a MetaMask wallet yet, create a new account and safely store the recovery phrase.



Step 3: Balances and Assets


• Make sure you have a native balance of coins or other crypto assets in your MetaMask wallet to use for transactions on PepeXchange.



Step 4: Access PepeXchange


• Open the PepeXchange website in your browser.



Step 5: Connect Your Wallet


• On the PepeXchange site, look for the “Connect Wallet” option or something similar.



Step 6: Allow Wallet Access


• MetaMask will ask for permission to interact with the PepeXchange site.

• Allow access.



Step 7: Choose a Blockchain Network


• To perform appropriate network settings, follow these steps:

• In MetaMask, click the network icon at the top right.

• Choose a network that is compatible with PepeXchange (for example, OPBNB, Smart Chain or others).



Step 8: Add Custom RPC (Optional)


• If you are using a custom blockchain network that is not on MetaMask's preferred list, you can add it as follows:

• Click "New RPC" (New RPC).

• Enter custom network details, including RPC URL, currency symbol, and more.



Step 9: Confirm Settings


• Make sure you have selected the appropriate network and confirm your network settings.



Step 10: Connection Successful


• After confirming the settings, your wallet is now connected to PepeXchange on the appropriate network.




Now you can use liquidity and trade crypto assets on PepeXchange by using the MetaMask wallet.

Make sure to keep your wallet recovery phrase safe and keep your wallet safe.
