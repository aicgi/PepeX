The following is a tutorial on how to claim an airdrop manually and this is only available manually



Step 1: Select a Crypto Wallet


The first step to getting started with PepeX is to choose the crypto wallet you will use. You can use a wallet like MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, or a hardware wallet like Ledger. Make sure your wallet supports blockchain networks that are compatible with PepeX, according to the available networks.



Step 2: Fill Your Wallet


Once you have a crypto wallet installed, make sure you have crypto assets in it. You need these assets to trade on PepeXchange. You can buy crypto assets on crypto exchanges such as Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken, and then transfer them to your wallet.



Step 3: Access To DEX


Visit the official website of PepeXchange via your web browser. Make sure you are connected to your crypto wallet.



Step 4: Choose an Asset Pair and Make a Swap


You can choose the crypto asset pair you want to trade. For example, the ETH/USDT, BNB/USDC pairs allow you to exchange Ethereum for Tether or OpBNB for USDC. Or choose the asset pair that suits your wishes and perform the swap as explained previously.



Step 5: Become a Liquidity Provider (Optional)


Apart from trading, you can also become a liquidity provider at PepeXchange. This means you provide your crypto assets to a liquidity pool so that other traders can trade them. To become a liquidity provider, select the appropriate asset pair, enter the amount of assets you want to add to the pool, and confirm the transaction.



Step 6: Wait for Confirmation


After confirmation, you have to wait until the transaction is processed on the appropriate blockchain network. The time required for confirmation may vary depending on the level of network congestion.



Step 7: Check Swap Results and Liquidity


Once the transaction is complete, you can check your wallet to ensure your new assets have been added and, if you are a liquidity provider, you can monitor your liquidity performance.




Vital Records !:

Make sure you understand the gas fees involved in transactions on the blockchain network you use.

Never give your wallet recovery phrase or personal information to anyone who tries to ask for your personal information.

This is a beginner's guide to getting started with PepeX, both as a trader and as a liquidity provider.
